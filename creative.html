A Few Years After Jubilee (2025)
A speculative radio project built with Fred Wordie for the EU Policy Lab, questioning the linearity of time & imagining a future where we have more space, to breathe, to experiment, to connect.

The Algorithm (2024)
An interactive simulation, built with Lujain Ibrahim, that demystifies social media recommendation algorithms.

Funded by Mozilla Foundation, winner of the Mozilla Creative Media Award, supported by IDFA DocLab.

Space Debris Game (2022)
Contributed game design to a Washington Post article and game on the growing space junk problem, built by the Lede Lab.

Multiplicity (2021)
A curation of articles about the internet, built with Lujain Ibrahim, Tom Abi Samra, and a collective of writers, designers, and editors.

Featured by the Webby Awards Responsible Tech Trends report.

Survival of the Best Fit (2019)
An educational game, built with Gabor Csapo, Jihyun Kim, and Miha Klasinc, on systemic bias in automated decision making systems, illustrated through AI use in hiring.

Funded by the Mozilla Foundation, winner of the Mozilla Creative Media Award.
