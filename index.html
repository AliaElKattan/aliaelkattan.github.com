I'm Alia, an Egyptian, Brooklyn-based PhD candidate at NYU Politics, and co-lead and creative technologist at Decifer Studio.

Currently, I'm a visiting PhD student at Centrum Wiskunde and Informatica (CWI), the Dutch national institute for computer science and math, based at the Distributed and Interactive Systems (DIS) lab.

I research how technology mediates how we learn about and participate in politics, and how to build safe and responsible human interaction with emerging technologies. I build interactive experiences about the impact of technology on society for broad audiences, and experiment with creative storytelling.
